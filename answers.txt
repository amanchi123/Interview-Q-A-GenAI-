What progress has been made in terms of promoting healthy lives and well-being for all ages?\nAnswer: In the 25 years before the Sustainable Development Goals were established, there was significant progress in promoting healthy lives and well-being for all ages. Preventable child deaths dropped by more than half, and maternal mortality also decreased significantly. However, challenges remain, such as the fact that 6 million children die every year before their fifth birthday, and AIDS is still the leading cause of death for adolescents in sub-Saharan Africa. The Sustainable Development Goals aim to build on these achievements and ensure good health for everyone, making it more than just a wish.\n--------------------------------------------------\n\nQuestion: 5. The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) related to education aim to ensure inclusive and equitable quality education and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all. The goals include achieving universal primary and secondary education, affordable vocational training, and access to higher education for all. The SDGs also focus on achieving gender equality in education and ending discrimination against women and girls in educational opportunities.\n--------------------------------------------------\n\nQuestion: 6. The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) aim to address these issues by building on achievements to ensure an end to discrimination against women and girls everywhere. The SDGs focus on promoting gender equality and empowering all women and girls to create a more inclusive and fair society.\n--------------------------------------------------\n\nQuestion: 7. The goal related to this in the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) is to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all by 2030.\n--------------------------------------------------\n\nQuestion: 8. How does economic growth tie into the goal of achieving decent work for all by 2030?\nAnswer: Economic growth plays a crucial role in achieving the goal of decent work for all by 2030. Additionally, economic growth can help in eradicating forced labor, slavery, and human trafficking, which are obstacles to achieving decent work for all.
